Throughout the programme the NIS-ITA had a Consortium Management Committee ("CMC") which consisted of a representative from each member organisation. The CMC was chaired by the representative from the Consortium Lead (IBM). Each member organisation had one voting representative on the CMC to support programmatic and management-related activities and decisions. The CMC made recommendations concerning the membership of the Consortium, the definition of tasks and goals of the member organisations, and the distribution of funding to the member organisations. Quarterly meetings were conducted by the CMC.
